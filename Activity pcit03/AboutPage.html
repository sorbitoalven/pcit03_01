College History

The Central Philippines State University is dubbed as the first Agricultural Institution in the countrty established by a Filipino Superintendent in the same of Professor Jose F. Crisanto immediately after World War II. CPSU is located 97 kilometers to Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental in the southeast and 103 km to Bacolod City in the North Sprawling in a 4,653.7-hectare land reservation, 19 kilometers from Kabankalan City, the Central Phillipines State University (CPSU).

Vision

We shall be among the global leaders in providing innovative quality education and training 2030.

Mission

We provide quality education and training through the use of innovative resources in instruction research and extension to improve the quality of life of students and other stakeholders.
